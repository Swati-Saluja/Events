Get to Know Events

Events is a digital media company connecting consumers with entertainment, movies and local events. Anyone can rely on Events to find out what's happening and decide what to do; from movies, concerts and sports to family fun and nightlife. With millions of local events to choose from, Events provides the world's most comprehensive selection of local entertainment content and serves its users across multiple platforms including online, mobile and email, delivering highly personalized content and recommendations to each of Eventsâ€™ registered users. Events' rating system empowers fans to make an event popular in an area. They can choose to give a higher rating or a lower rating as per their liking, and get an instant feedback from other users also, about the events based on their ratings. The users can instantly find events in nearby locations, also they can get a list of the upcoming events based on their location search. Events save user preferences based on their previous searches so that the user doesn't have to fill-in the search fields again and again and get the search results instantly as soon as they enter the site.
